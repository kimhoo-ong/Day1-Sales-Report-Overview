**Analyst:** Kim Hoo

---

## What Is This?

This repository documents a **30-day Power BI dashboard challenge** — one complete, production-quality dashboard built and published every day for 30 consecutive days.

Each day covers a different industry, dataset, and analytical problem. Every dashboard follows a senior analyst workflow: data discovery, profiling, insight mining, requirements specification, build, and publish.

---

## The Analyst Workflow (Applied Every Day)

| Step | Phase | Output |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Data Discovery** | Load raw data. Check shape, dtypes, nulls, date range. Never assume. |
| 2 | **Data Profiling** | Descriptive stats on every column. Identify outliers and anomalies. |
| 3 | **Business Context** | Who uses this? What decisions do they make? What drives action? |
| 4 | **Insight Mining** | Slice by every dimension. Find anomalies, rankings, trends, correlations. |
| 5 | **Dashboard Requirements** | Define KPIs, charts, filters, layout — before opening Power BI. |
| 6 | **Data Preparation** | Clean, transform, model. Build calculated columns and DAX measures. |
| 7 | **Build & Iterate** | Build, review, refine. Never ship v1 without a review. |
